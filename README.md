*/Overview

This repository presents my Digital Literacy Project, developed as part of my academic curriculum.

The project aims to enhance awareness of responsible digital practices, online safety, and effective communication in the modern digital environment. It highlights the importance of using technology in a safe, ethical, and productive manner for learning, collaboration, and professional growth.

***Objectives
To understand the significance of digital literacy in today’s world
To promote responsible and secure use of the internet
To identify and analyze common cyber threats
To develop effective digital communication practices
To spread awareness among students regarding safe online behavior

## Project Components

The repository includes the following key elements:

1) Cybercrime Case Study
2) Infographic / Awareness Material
3) Do’s and Don’ts Guidelines
4) Digital Communication Scenario Analysis
5) Cybercrime Focus: Cyberbullying

This project specifically examines cyberbullying as a major digital threat.

## Case Insight

Digital communication, when not handled carefully, can lead to misunderstandings and unintended consequences. For instance, a sarcastic or insensitive comment posted online may harm others and negatively impact relationships and reputation.


**Preventive Measures
Practice clear and respectful communication
Avoid impulsive posting
Be mindful of tone and audience
Promote positive and supportive interactions online
